So all other review sites suck, since you either can't review all things, they use a shitty limited scoring system, or what have you. So here we are!
If a review doesn't have a review date, it's an old review I made too long ago, and it might not be accurate to how I feel anymore.
If a review doesn't have a score, I haven't watched/read/played/listened to/etc it yet. Basically a TODO list.

Just so everyone is on the same page, this is the scale I use:

10- The best thing ever. No faults, nothing I would have done different personally
5- Incredibly average. Didn't like it, didn't dislike it. See: Avatar (2009)
0- The worst thing ever. Everyone involved should be in jail
