Beta 0.11

Put YouTube links in the input boxes, then hit "Play" on the video you want to play to start.
Press '+' to get more slots, and '-' to remove that slot.
Use the drop down menu to select what kind of looping to do.

GET parameters:
autoplay=[index] to autoplay that index
DEFAULT_NO_RANDOM=[whatever] to have the top secret default playlist not be random
