Put YouTube links (or just video IDs) in the input boxes, then hit "Play".

GET parameters:
load=[index] to load that index on page load
DEFAULT_NO_RANDOM to have the top secret default playlist not be random

Keyboard controls:
Left/right arrow keys to go to next/previous video
k to toggle playing current video
r to replay current video
